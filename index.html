The 05th Tanzania Annual ICT Conference 2021.

About the Conference.

The Tanzania Annual ICT Conference 2021 (TAIC-2021) is a platform for discussion, networking and raising aware- ness on the global ICT affairs, business opportunities, innovation and skills needed to adapt with the dynamic digital economy. The Conference also serves as the platform for participants to discuss on enabling environment aspects for effective use of ICT as an enabling tool towards socio-economic development in Tanzania. The confer- ence brings together more than 500 ICT stakeholders from around the World, Government and Private Sector, Small Medium Enterprises, Academia, ICT Entrepreneurs and the business industry.

This year’s conference will also have an additional feature of contributing to academic rewards in Higher Learn- ing Institutions by producing a referred conference proceedings. All submitted papers will be double blinded peer reviewed by competent reviewers. Accepted papers will be published in TAIC 2021 conference proceedings with ISSN, which can be accessed online.
